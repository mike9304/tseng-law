## Homepage Section Order (Wireframe-Level)
1. Hero
   - Headline + subheadline
   - Global search bar
   - Recommended keyword chips
   - Recommended categories (quick links)
   - Secondary links: Browse Services / Contact
2. Featured
   - "Top Highlights" horizontal scroll-snap carousel
   - 1 lead card + 2–3 secondary cards
3. Services Snapshot
   - Bento grid of key practice areas
   - Small text intro + quick CTA to Services
4. Resource Lists
   - Two-column lists: services + external channels
5. Profile / Updates Multi-List
   - Three-column lists: education / experience / practice areas
6. Video Channel
   - 1 featured video + 4 thumbnails
   - Link to /videos
7. Contact CTA
   - Calm, professional contact block

## Option Placement Rules
- Search: Always in header; primary hero search at top of homepage.
- Keyword chips: Directly under the hero search, before any other modules.
- Recommended categories: Under keyword chips in hero.
- CTAs: Primary CTA in header; secondary CTAs at the end of hero and in service blocks.
- Filters/tabs: Inside their module headers when used.
- Section labels: Above section titles (small caps), acting as anchors.
